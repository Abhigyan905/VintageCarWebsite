About the 1957 Chevrolet Corvette

The **1957 Chevrolet Corvette** is a true American icon, featuring a **sleek fiberglass body**, **powerful V8 engine**, and a design that defined the golden era of sports cars. With its lightweight build and racing pedigree, it remains one of the most collectible Corvettes ever made.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1957
  • Engine: 4.6L V8
  • Horsepower: 283 HP
  • Top Speed: 132 mph (212 km/h)
  • Transmission: 3-Speed Manual / Optional 4-Speed
  • Body Style: Convertible
