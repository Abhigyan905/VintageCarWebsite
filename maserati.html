About the Maserati 250F

The **Maserati 250F** was a dominant force in **Formula 1 racing during the 1950s**. Driven by legends like **Juan Manuel Fangio**, it remains one of the most beautiful and legendary racing cars of all time.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1954 - 1958
  • Engine: 2.5L Inline-6
  • Horsepower: 270 HP
  • Top Speed: 180 mph (290 km/h)
  • Transmission: 5-Speed Manual
  • Weight: 650 kg
