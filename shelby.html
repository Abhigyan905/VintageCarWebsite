About the Shelby Cobra 427

The **Shelby Cobra 427** is a legendary **American muscle roadster** built in the 1960s. Developed by Carroll Shelby, it combined a **lightweight British chassis** with a **massive 7.0L Ford V8 engine**, creating one of the most iconic and powerful muscle cars ever.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1965 - 1967
  • Engine: 7.0L (427ci) Ford V8
  • Horsepower: 425 HP
  • Top Speed: 164 mph (264 km/h)
  • Transmission: 4-Speed Manual
  • 0-60 mph: 4.2 seconds
