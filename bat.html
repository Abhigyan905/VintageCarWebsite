About the 1989 Batmobile

The **1989 Batmobile**, driven by **Michael Keaton’s Batman**, is one of the most iconic vehicles in cinema history. With its **sleek jet-inspired design**, **machine guns**, and **flame-throwing afterburner**, it was the **perfect vehicle for Gotham’s Dark Knight**.

Specifications

  • Base Model: Chevrolet Impala Chassis
  • Engine: 5.7L V8
  • Horsepower: 380 HP
  • Top Speed: 330 mph (530 km/h) (Fictional)
  • Gadgets: Machine Guns, Grappling Hook, Bat Disc Launchers
  • Afterburner: Jet Engine-Powered Booster
