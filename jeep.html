About the Jeep Willys

The **Jeep Willys**, first introduced in **1941**, became the backbone of Allied forces in World War II. Its **rugged build, 4x4 capability, and reliability** made it a symbol of adventure. Even today, it remains a **legendary off-roader**, cherished by collectors and enthusiasts alike.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1941 - 1983
  • Engine: 2.2L Inline-4 "Go-Devil"
  • Horsepower: 60 HP
  • Top Speed: 65 mph (105 km/h)
  • Transmission: 3-Speed Manual
  • Drivetrain: 4x4
