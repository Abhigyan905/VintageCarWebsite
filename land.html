About the Land Rover Series 1

The **Land Rover Series 1**, launched in **1948**, was the first of its kind, designed for both **agricultural and military use**. With **unmatched durability**, it became the foundation for the iconic Land Rover legacy. Even today, it's a **sought-after classic** for collectors and off-road enthusiasts.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1948 - 1958
  • Engine: 1.6L & 2.0L Inline-4
  • Horsepower: 50-52 HP
  • Top Speed: 55 mph (88 km/h)
  • Transmission: 4-Speed Manual
  • Drivetrain: 4x4 with Low Range Gears
