About the Chevrolet Camaro Z/28

The **Chevrolet Camaro Z/28**, first introduced in **1967**, was designed as a high-performance pony car. With its **5.0L V8 engine**, it became a serious competitor in Trans-Am racing and remains one of the most beloved muscle cars.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1967 - 2002
  • Engine: 5.0L (302ci) V8
  • Horsepower: 290 HP
  • Top Speed: 130 mph (209 km/h)
  • Transmission: 4-Speed Manual
  • 0-60 mph: 7.4 seconds
