About the Ecto-1

The **Ecto-1** is the iconic ghost-hunting vehicle from *Ghostbusters*, based on a **1959 Cadillac Miller-Meteor** ambulance. With its unique siren, flashing lights, and paranormal equipment, it's become one of the most recognizable cars in film history. Whether chasing ghosts or speeding through New York City, the Ecto-1 remains a **fan-favorite classic**.

Specifications

  • Base Model: 1959 Cadillac Miller-Meteor
  • Engine: 6.3L V8
  • Horsepower: 320 HP
  • Transmission: 4-Speed Automatic
  • Special Features: Sirens, Roof-Mounted Proton Pack, Ghost Containment Unit
