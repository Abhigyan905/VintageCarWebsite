About the Porsche 904 Carrera RS

The **Porsche 904 Carrera RS**, introduced in **1964**, was designed as a lightweight racing car that dominated endurance events. With its **fiberglass body** and **mid-engine layout**, it was a masterpiece of **engineering and performance**.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1964 - 1965
  • Engine: 2.0L Flat-4 / Flat-6
  • Horsepower: 180 - 200 HP
  • Top Speed: 160 mph (257 km/h)
  • Transmission: 5-Speed Manual
  • Weight: 650 kg
