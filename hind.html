About the Hindustan Ambassador

The **Hindustan Ambassador**, introduced in **1957**, is regarded as **India’s most iconic car**. Based on the **Morris Oxford Series III**, it was the preferred choice of politicians, diplomats, and families for decades. Known for its **durability, spacious interior, and classic design**, it remained in production until **2014**.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1957 - 2014
  • Engine Options: 1.5L Petrol / 2.0L Diesel
  • Horsepower: 50 - 75 HP
  • Top Speed: 120 km/h
  • Transmission: 4-Speed Manual
  • Body Style: 4-Door Sedan
