About the Jaguar E-Type

The **Jaguar E-Type**, introduced in **1961**, is often hailed as one of the most beautiful cars ever built. With its **sleek aerodynamics**, **powerful inline-6 engine**, and **lightweight chassis**, it offered unmatched performance and elegance in its era. Even Enzo Ferrari once called it "the most beautiful car ever made."
