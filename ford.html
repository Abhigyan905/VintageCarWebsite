About the Ford GT40

The Ford GT40 was built to dominate Ferrari at Le Mans, and it did, winning four consecutive times from 1966-1969.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1964 - 1969
  • Engine: 7.0L V8
  • Horsepower: 485 HP
  • Top Speed: 200 mph (322 km/h)
  • Transmission: 5-Speed Manual
