About the Mercedes-Benz 300SL Roadster

The **Mercedes-Benz 300SL Roadster**, introduced in **1957**, was the convertible successor to the famous **Gullwing Coupe**. Renowned for its **stunning design**, **cutting-edge fuel injection system**, and **lightweight tubular frame**, it remains one of the most **sought-after classic cars** ever built.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1957 - 1963
  • Engine: 3.0L Inline-6
  • Horsepower: 215 HP
  • Top Speed: 155 mph (249 km/h)
  • Transmission: 4-Speed Manual
  • Body Style: Convertible / Roadster
