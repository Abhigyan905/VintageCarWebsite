About the Premier Padmini

The **Premier Padmini**, launched in **1964**, was India's most beloved city car. Based on the **Fiat 1100**, it was widely used as a **family car and Mumbai taxi**. Known for its **smooth ride, stylish design, and compact size**, it remained a part of India's streets for decades.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1964 - 2000
  • Engine: 1.1L Inline-4
  • Horsepower: 40 - 50 HP
  • Top Speed: 120 km/h
  • Transmission: 4-Speed Manual
  • Body Style: 4-Door Sedan
