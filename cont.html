About the Hindustan Contessa

The **Hindustan Contessa**, launched in **1984**, was India's first muscle sedan. Based on the **Vauxhall Victor FE**, it became a symbol of **luxury and power**. Known for its **smooth performance, premium interiors, and bold design**, it was one of India's finest cars.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1984 - 2002
  • Engine: 1.8L & 2.0L Inline-4
  • Horsepower: 88 - 110 HP
  • Top Speed: 140 km/h
  • Transmission: 4-Speed / 5-Speed Manual
  • Body Style: 4-Door Sedan
