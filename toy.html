About the Toyota Land Cruiser FJ40

The **Toyota Land Cruiser FJ40**, introduced in **1960**, is an off-road icon known for its **rugged build**, **reliability**, and **go-anywhere attitude**. From **deserts to jungles**, this legendary 4x4 has conquered every terrain and remains a **collector’s favorite** worldwide.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1960 - 1984
  • Engine: 3.9L & 4.2L Inline-6
  • Horsepower: 125 - 135 HP
  • Top Speed: 75 mph (121 km/h)
  • Transmission: 3 or 4-Speed Manual
  • Drivetrain: 4x4 with Low Range
