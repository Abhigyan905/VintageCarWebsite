About the DeLorean DMC-12

The **DeLorean DMC-12**, built between **1981 and 1983**, became a pop culture icon after starring in the **Back to the Future** movies. With its **gull-wing doors**, **stainless steel body**, and futuristic look, it remains one of the most recognizable cars in history.

Specifications

  • Year of Production: 1981 - 1983
  • Engine: 2.85L V6
  • Horsepower: 130 HP
  • Top Speed: 110 mph (177 km/h)
  • Transmission: 5-Speed Manual / 3-Speed Auto
  • Doors: Gull-Wing
